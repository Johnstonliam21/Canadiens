NHL News

Today's topic is going to be the Edmonton Oilers. The Edmonton Oilers are an organization that has been at the bottom of the leaderboards for some years. They have gotten a lot of top picks like Taylor Hall being the #1 pick in 2010, Ryan Nugent-Hopkins being the #1 pick in 2011, Nail Yakupov being the #1 pick in 2012, Darnell Nurse being the #7 pick in 2013, Leon Draisaitl being the #3 pick in 2014, CONNOR MCDAVID being the #1 pick in 2015 and Jesse Puljujarvi being the #4 pick in 2016. But they always managed to stay at the bottom even last year with Connor McDavid, the best player in the league!!! But this year that has all changed, not only are they not at the bottom, they are actually in a playoff spot. Even while letting go of Nail Yakupov and Taylor Hall, they are having one of their best seasons in a while. Who thinks that Connor McDavid is the best player in the NHL and who thinks that they have a chance at winning the Stanley cup this year.
